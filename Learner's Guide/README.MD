# **ğŸ“˜ 7. Responsible & Ethical AI Use**

### **1. Protect personal information**

Never share sensitive data with AI:

- Addresses, phone numbers, passwords
- Identification numbers
- Medical information
- Private personal content

### **2. Watch for bias**

AI reflects patterns in the data it was trained on. Be critical of AI outputs in:

- Hiring and school admissions
- Health advice or legal guidance
- Content moderation

### **3. Fact-check information**

AI can make mistakes. Always verify important information using **trusted sources**.

### **4. Use AI to learn, not cheat**

AI can assist in studying or completing tasks, but **your understanding comes from your own thinking**.

**Activity:** Pick one recent AI interaction and **analyze its reliability, fairness, and usefulness**.
