### **Section 5: Best Practices for Integrating AI in Sales Teams**

**5.1 Tips for Successfully Integrating AI**

* **Start Small**: Begin by using one AI tool to address a specific challenge (e.g., lead scoring or sales forecasting).
* **Augment, Don’t Replace**: AI should enhance your sales efforts, not replace your expertise. Use AI for automation and insight, but rely on your judgment for relationship-building and strategy.
* **Continuously Monitor and Adjust**: Regularly check the effectiveness of AI tools and make adjustments as needed.

**Activity 6: Create Your Action Plan for Implementing AI**
