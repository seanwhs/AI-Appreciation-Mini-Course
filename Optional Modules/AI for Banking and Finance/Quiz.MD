## **Section 3 ‚Äî Short Answer (5 Questions)**

1. Name two AI tools used for **fraud detection**.
   **Sample Answer:** Darktrace, FICO Falcon

2. How does AI assist in **credit risk assessment**?
   **Sample Answer:** Predicts default likelihood, evaluates creditworthiness beyond traditional scores, recommends optimal loan terms

3. Name one AI application in **algorithmic trading or investment management**.
   **Sample Answer:** BlackRock Aladdin, Kavout, QuantConnect

4. How can AI enhance **customer service in banking**?
   **Sample Answer:** Automates responses, handles routine queries, detects sentiment, escalates complex issues to humans

5. Why is AI beneficial for **regulatory compliance and reporting**?
   **Sample Answer:** Monitors transactions for breaches, generates reports automatically, tracks evolving regulations, flags anomalies

## **Section 4 ‚Äî Application / Scenario (3 Questions)**

1. A retail bank notices unusual credit card patterns. How could AI help detect fraud?
   **Sample Answer:** Analyze transaction patterns, flag anomalies, reduce false positives, and alert fraud analysts in real time

2. A lending firm wants to approve loans faster while managing risk. How might AI be applied?
   **Sample Answer:** Use predictive models to score applicants, segment by risk tier, recommend loan terms, monitor portfolio risk

3. An investment manager wants to stress-test a portfolio against market volatility. How can agentic AI assist?
   **Sample Answer:** Simulate market scenarios, forecast portfolio performance under stress, suggest rebalancing strategies, evaluate risk-adjusted returns
