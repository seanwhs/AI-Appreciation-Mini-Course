# **2. AI in Banking & Finance ‚Äî The Big Picture**

Banking and finance are AI-ready because they rely on:

* Large structured datasets (transactions, accounts, loans)
* Unstructured data (news, call transcripts, social media)
* Risk analysis, fraud detection, regulatory compliance
* Predictive analytics for lending, investments, and customer behavior
* Repetitive operational workflows

**AI automates:** Routine, high-volume, rules-based tasks (transaction monitoring, credit scoring, reporting)
**Humans handle:** Strategic decisions, customer relationships, compliance interpretation, ethics

# **3. Where AI Shows Up in Banking & Finance**

---

## **A. Fraud Detection & AML**

**AI does:** Real-time transaction monitoring, anomaly detection, network analysis
**Use Cases:** Suspicious wire transfers, unusual card activity, AML compliance checks
**Tools:** Darktrace, FICO Falcon, SAS Fraud Framework, Featurespace ARIC

**Reflection:**
‚úèÔ∏è How could AI reduce false positives while improving detection speed?

---

---

## **B. Credit Scoring & Risk Assessment**

**AI does:** Enhanced credit scoring, default prediction, dynamic portfolio risk monitoring
**Use Cases:** Small business loans, borrower default probability, credit line optimization
**Tools:** Zest AI, Upstart, Experian AI, FICO Analytics

---

## **C. Algorithmic Trading & Investment Management**

**AI does:** Market analysis, trading signals, portfolio optimization, robo-advisory support
**Use Cases:** High-frequency trading, asset price prediction, auto-rebalancing portfolios
**Tools:** Kavout, QuantConnect, Alpaca AI, BlackRock Aladdin

---

## **D. Customer Service & Virtual Assistants**

**AI does:** Chatbots, voice assistants, sentiment detection, automated escalation
**Use Cases:** Banking app support, investment advice assistants, call center automation
**Tools:** Kasisto KAI, Nuance Virtual Assistants, IBM Watson Assistant

---

## **E. Regulatory Compliance & Reporting**

**AI does:** Compliance monitoring, automatic report generation, anomaly detection
**Use Cases:** AML/KYC checks, Basel III / IFRS 9 reporting, trade compliance monitoring
**Tools:** Ayasdi AML, ComplyAdvantage, Workiva AI, Ascent RegTech

---

## **F. Predictive Analytics & Financial Forecasting**

**AI does:** Cash flow forecasting, revenue projection, credit risk early-warning signals
**Use Cases:** Predict loan defaults, forecast FX or interest rates, anticipate liquidity needs
**Tools:** IBM Watson FS, SAS Analytics, Alteryx, FICO Analytic Cloud

---

## **G. Agentic AI for Simulation & Scenario Testing**

**AI does:** Generates market and transaction scenarios, stress-tests portfolios, simulates operational risks
**Use Cases:** Bank capital stress testing, synthetic fraud data generation, strategy testing
**Tools:** AnyLogic Financial Simulation, MITRE Caldera AI (finance adaptation), Numerix Scenario Simulator

### **Case Study 1: Fraud Detection & Transaction Monitoring**

*Detect suspicious credit card activity across thousands of accounts.*

**Dataset Columns:**

```
Transaction_ID, Account_ID, Transaction_Amount, Transaction_Type, Timestamp, Location, Merchant_Category, Flagged_Fraud
```

**Practice Tasks:**

* Identify potential fraudulent transactions
* Analyze anomalous patterns
* Suggest improvements to AI detection models

---

### **Case Study 2: Credit Scoring & Loan Risk**

*Improve loan approvals using AI-driven risk assessment.*

**Dataset Columns:**

```
Applicant_ID, Age, Income, Credit_History, Debt_to_Income_Ratio, Loan_Amount, Loan_Term, Defaulted, Predicted_Risk_Score
```

**Practice Tasks:**

* Predict default probability
* Segment applicants by risk tier
* Recommend loan approval or conditions

---

### **Case Study 3: Portfolio Optimization & Market Simulation**

*Simulate market volatility impacts on client portfolios.*

**Dataset Columns:**

```
Portfolio_ID, Asset_Class, Market_Value, Expected_Return, Volatility, Correlation, Predicted_Risk
```

**Practice Tasks:**

* Simulate performance under market stress
* Suggest rebalancing strategies
* Evaluate risk-adjusted returns
