# **AI for Banking & Finance — Task Suitability Matrix**

| **Banking / Finance Task**                    | **AI Potential** | **Notes / Examples**                                                                       |
| --------------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Fraud Detection & AML                         | High             | Real-time monitoring, anomaly detection, network analysis of transactions                  |
| Credit Scoring & Risk Assessment              | High             | Predict default probability, segment applicants, optimize loan terms                       |
| Algorithmic Trading & Investment Management   | High             | Predictive trading signals, portfolio optimization, auto-rebalancing                       |
| Customer Service & Virtual Assistants         | High             | Chatbots, sentiment detection, routine inquiry handling                                    |
| Regulatory Compliance & Reporting             | Medium-High      | Automates AML/KYC checks, regulatory report generation, compliance monitoring              |
| Predictive Analytics & Financial Forecasting  | Medium-High      | Cash flow, market trends, portfolio risk forecasting                                       |
| Agentic AI Simulation & Scenario Testing      | Medium-High      | Stress tests, operational simulations, synthetic transaction generation                    |
| Manual Spreadsheet Analysis / Reporting       | Medium           | AI can assist in data cleanup, formula generation, trend analysis                          |
| Strategic Financial Decisions                 | Low              | Requires human judgment, market understanding, and ethical considerations                  |
| Customer Relationship & Advisory Services     | Low              | AI assists with recommendations, but human interaction and personalization remain critical |
| Regulatory Interpretation / Ethical Oversight | Low              | High stakes; AI supports monitoring but cannot replace professional interpretation         |

**Legend:**

* **High:** AI can largely automate or augment the task
* **Medium-High:** AI assists significantly but human review or decision needed
* **Medium:** AI supports the task, often for efficiency or error reduction
* **Low:** Human expertise is essential

✅ **How to Use This Matrix in Training**

1. **Task Mapping Activity:** Learners list their daily banking or finance tasks, then map each to **AI Potential** using the matrix.
2. **Tool Alignment:** Match tasks with recommended AI tools for hands-on exercises.
3. **Prioritization:** Focus on **High AI Potential** tasks first to maximize efficiency and accuracy gains.
4. **Critical Thinking:** Discuss **Low AI Potential** tasks where human judgment, ethics, and strategic insight are essential.
5. **Simulation Exercises:** Use agentic AI and predictive analytics to test scenarios without risking real assets.
