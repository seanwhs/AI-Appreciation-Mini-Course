# **2. AI: What It Does vs. What Humans Do Best**

# ----------------------------------------------------

### **AI Excels at:**

* Repetitive, rules-based tasks
* Pattern recognition
* Data summarization
* Generating drafts of text, visuals, and ideas
* Predicting outcomes from large datasets

### **Humans Excel at:**

* Navigating ambiguity
* Judgment, ethics & intuition
* Creativity and originality
* Emotional intelligence & empathy
* Leadership and relationship building
