# **AI & the Future of Work — The Big Picture**

Artificial intelligence is rapidly becoming a foundational layer across industries. It:

* Automates routine, rules-based, high-volume tasks
* Supports decision-making with real-time insights
* Reduces errors and accelerates workflows
* Unlocks new forms of creativity, analysis, and problem-solving
* Enables entirely new categories of jobs, tools, and professions

But despite its power, **AI does not replace humans wholesale**.
Instead, it reshapes what humans are *best at*.

### **AI excels at:**

* Predictable, repeatable, structured tasks
* Pattern recognition at scale
* Data summarization and retrieval
* Drafting content, analyzing trends, and generating options

### **Humans excel at:**

* Navigating ambiguity
* Judgment under uncertainty
* Ethical decision-making
* Creativity, originality, and intuition
* Relationship building, empathy, and leadership
* Cross-disciplinary problem framing

The most successful workers will be those who become **AI-augmented**—using AI as a force multiplier for their human strengths.

# **1. Administration, Operations & Support Roles**

AI is transforming the backbone of office work by eliminating routine bottlenecks.

### **What AI Does**

* Responds to emails and drafts communication
* Handles scheduling, reminders, and meeting preparation
* Summarizes documents, notes, and conversations
* Manages internal & external Q&A via chatbots
* Automates repetitive clerical and operational workflows

### **Use Cases**

* Auto-generated meeting notes and action items
* Smart triage for help desks and ticketing systems
* Inbox management and email drafting
* Document organization, extraction, and summarization

### **Tools**

Microsoft 365 Copilot • Google Duet AI • Zendesk AI • Intercom Fin • Notion AI

### **Human Role**

* Context-driven decisions
* Escalation management
* Relationship maintenance
* Communication requiring nuance
* Quality control and oversight

# **Activity — “AI Futures Map for Your Career”**

Learners will:

1. List **5–7 tasks** from their current role
2. Rate each task on:

   * Repetition
   * Predictability
   * Rules-based structure
   * Time volume
   * Value to the business
3. Categorize tasks into:

   * **Automate** (AI can handle most or all)
   * **Augment** (AI assists, humans refine)
   * **Human-led** (judgment, strategy, creativity required)
4. Design a **future-ready role description** integrating AI as a collaborator

This creates a personal blueprint for career evolution and self-reinvention.
