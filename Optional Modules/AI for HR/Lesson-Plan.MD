# **Lesson Plan: AI for HR**

## **Objective:**

Equip HR professionals and learners to understand the practical applications of AI in HR functions such as recruitment, employee engagement, performance management, and workforce analytics. By the end of the session, participants will be able to recognize AI’s role in HR processes, evaluate AI tools, and implement AI solutions in HR workflows.

---

## **Session Duration:**

**Total Time:** 2 hours
**Breakdown:**

* Introduction: 10 minutes
* Core Topics (AI in HR Functions): 1 hour 15 minutes
* Hands-on Activity: 20 minutes
* Wrap-up & Q&A: 15 minutes

---

## **Materials Needed:**

* Laptop/Projector for presentation
* Access to AI tools (demo or trial version where applicable)
* Handouts (AI for HR Cheat Sheet)
* Whiteboard/Flipchart for brainstorming activities
* Sample datasets (Excel files or HRMS logs)
* Case studies for role-playing

---

## **Lesson Plan Breakdown:**

### **1. Introduction to AI in HR (10 minutes)**

**Objective:** Introduce learners to the concept of AI in HR and the overall goal of the module.
**Activities:**

* Quick discussion on AI’s impact across industries.
* Brief overview of the potential of AI in HR.
* Explain the objectives of the session:

  * Understanding where AI is embedded in HR workflows.
  * Exploring AI tools for recruitment, employee engagement, performance management, etc.
  * Hands-on practice using AI-powered HR tools.

---

### **2. Core Topic 1: Talent Acquisition & Recruitment (20 minutes)**

**Objective:** Understand how AI can automate and enhance the recruitment process.
**Activities:**

* **Presentation:** AI’s role in talent acquisition (resume screening, candidate matching, etc.).
* **Real-Life Use Cases:**

  * Automating resume screening using AI-based tools (e.g., HireVue, Ideal, X0PA AI).
  * AI in interview scheduling and predictive hiring.
* **Tool Demonstration/Discussion:**

  * Show a brief demo of **HireVue** or **X0PA AI** for candidate screening.
  * Discuss benefits: reduced bias, faster screening, improved matching.

**Discussion Prompt:**

* How could AI reduce time spent on manual resume screening?

---

### **3. Core Topic 2: Employee Engagement & Experience (20 minutes)**

**Objective:** Learn how AI enhances employee engagement through predictive analytics and feedback systems.
**Activities:**

* **Presentation:** AI in employee engagement (sentiment analysis, turnover prediction).
* **Real-Life Use Cases:**

  * Sentiment analysis from employee surveys (e.g., CultureAmp, Peakon).
  * Predicting turnover risks and implementing retention strategies.
* **Tool Demonstration/Discussion:**

  * Show the AI-powered dashboards in **CultureAmp**.
  * Discuss how AI analyzes feedback and predicts employee disengagement.

**Discussion Prompt:**

* How can AI help personalize engagement strategies for individual employees?

---

### **4. Core Topic 3: Performance Management & Feedback (20 minutes)**

**Objective:** Explore how AI can automate performance tracking and feedback to drive employee development.
**Activities:**

* **Presentation:** AI in continuous performance tracking, automated feedback, and goal alignment.
* **Real-Life Use Cases:**

  * AI-driven performance review systems (e.g., Lattice, 15Five).
  * Real-time feedback and KPI monitoring.
* **Tool Demonstration/Discussion:**

  * Brief demo of **Lattice** or **15Five** for tracking employee performance and generating reports.
  * Discuss how AI optimizes feedback cycles and performance assessments.

**Discussion Prompt:**

* How does AI ensure that feedback is both timely and relevant to the employee's development?

---

### **5. Core Topic 4: HR Analytics & Workforce Planning (20 minutes)**

**Objective:** Understand how AI-driven analytics provide insights into employee trends and workforce planning.
**Activities:**

* **Presentation:** AI in HR analytics (predictive modeling, workforce planning).
* **Real-Life Use Cases:**

  * Predicting employee turnover (e.g., Visier, Workday).
  * AI-driven workforce planning based on predictive analytics.
* **Tool Demonstration/Discussion:**

  * Show how **Visier** or **Workday** can predict turnover and plan hiring strategies.
  * Discuss the potential of AI to forecast skill shortages or workforce needs.

**Discussion Prompt:**

* How can AI-driven workforce planning improve resource allocation and employee retention?

---

### **6. Core Topic 5: Payroll & Benefits Automation (15 minutes)**

**Objective:** Learn how AI simplifies payroll and benefits management.
**Activities:**

* **Presentation:** AI in payroll and benefits (automation, compliance, personalization).
* **Real-Life Use Cases:**

  * Automating payroll calculations and compliance tracking (e.g., Zenefits, Gusto).
  * AI-driven benefits administration and cost prediction.
* **Tool Demonstration/Discussion:**

  * Discuss payroll automation features in **Zenefits** or **Gusto**.
  * Explore benefits predictions using AI (e.g., reducing benefits costs).

**Discussion Prompt:**

* How can AI-driven benefits optimization help HR teams reduce administrative costs?

---

### **7. Hands-on Activity: Applying AI to HR Tasks (20 minutes)**

**Objective:** Give learners a chance to apply AI in real-world HR scenarios through a guided activity.
**Activity Breakdown:**

* **Instructions:** Participants will break into small groups and work through one of the following case studies, applying AI concepts learned.

  * **Case Study 1: Talent Acquisition:** Use a dataset to automate resume screening and candidate shortlisting.
  * **Case Study 2: Employee Engagement:** Analyze employee feedback data to predict engagement levels and suggest retention strategies.
  * **Case Study 3: Performance Management:** Track employee performance data and generate feedback for improvement.
* **Goal:** Evaluate how AI can optimize these HR tasks and provide actionable insights.

**Discussion Prompt for Groups:**

* After completing the activity, each group will present their findings and suggest AI-based improvements for HR workflows.

---

### **8. Wrap-Up and Q&A (15 minutes)**

**Objective:** Conclude the lesson and address any lingering questions.
**Activities:**

* **Recap:** Highlight key points learned during the session:

  * How AI impacts various HR functions (recruitment, engagement, performance management, etc.).
  * The benefits of AI in HR, from automation to personalized experiences.
* **Q&A:** Open the floor for any remaining questions or concerns.

**Closing Prompt:**

* How can you envision using AI to improve HR efficiency and decision-making at your organization?

---

## **Homework / Extended Learning:**

* **Assignment:** Research and write a brief report (1–2 pages) on an AI tool or platform in HR that interests you. Discuss its features, potential benefits, and any challenges related to implementation.
* **Optional Exercise:** Sign up for a demo of any AI-powered HR tool and test its features.

---

This lesson plan will help learners understand how AI can enhance various HR processes, from recruitment to performance management. Through discussions, demonstrations, and hands-on activities, they will gain the practical knowledge to integrate AI solutions into their HR practices.
