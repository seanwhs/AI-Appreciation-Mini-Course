### **Lesson Plan for AI in Retail — Expanded Instructor Version**

---

### **Module Title:** AI for Retail

### **Duration:** 3–4 hours

### **Goal of This Module:**

Equip learners to recognize:

* **Where AI already appears in retail workflows**
* **Which tools and platforms enable AI-assisted merchandising, operations, and customer engagement**
* **What tasks AI can automate, augment, or accelerate**
* **How retail professionals can partner with AI** to improve customer experiences, reduce errors, and enhance operational efficiency
* **How AI can assist in demand forecasting, inventory management, and personalized marketing**

---

### **Learning Objectives:**

By the end of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Understand how AI is transforming various aspects of retail, from inventory management to customer engagement.
2. Identify the AI tools and platforms used in retail workflows.
3. Apply AI techniques to improve demand forecasting, inventory optimization, personalized marketing, and fraud prevention.
4. Use AI to simulate retail operations, test marketing strategies, and improve customer experiences.
5. Understand the partnership between humans and AI in retail and how it drives operational efficiency.

---

### **Materials Needed:**

* Computers/tablets with access to AI tools (such as Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Blue Yonder, Trax Retail).
* Sample datasets (e.g., sales data, customer engagement, store traffic data).
* Whiteboard or projector for interactive discussions.
* Handouts: **AI in Retail Cheat Sheet**, **Sample Datasets**, **Case Study Materials**.
* Group activity worksheets for role-playing scenarios.

### **Module Breakdown:**

---

#### **1. Introduction to AI in Retail (30 minutes)**

**Objective:** Provide an overview of AI’s role in retail and its transformative potential.

1. **AI in Retail — The Big Picture:**

   * Retail is highly data-driven, making it a natural fit for AI implementation.
   * **AI automates:** Routine tasks such as inventory replenishment, demand forecasting, and customer segmentation.
   * **Humans handle:** Strategic decision-making, creative problem-solving, supplier negotiations, and customer relationship management.
   * AI is embedded in **sales, marketing, supply chain, and customer service**, enhancing precision, personalization, and operational efficiency.

2. **Key Areas Where AI Shows Up in Retail:**

   * **Inventory & Supply Chain Optimization** (forecasting demand, optimizing stock levels)
   * **Personalized Marketing & Customer Engagement** (personalized recommendations, dynamic pricing)
   * **Customer Service & Chatbots** (automated support, sentiment analysis)
   * **Fraud Detection & Loss Prevention** (anomaly detection, theft prevention)
   * **Demand Forecasting & Sales Analytics** (sales predictions, trend analysis)
   * **Visual Merchandising & Store Layout Optimization** (traffic analysis, product placement)
   * **Agentic AI for Simulation & Scenario Testing** (testing promotions, operational simulations)

---

#### **2. AI Tools & Platforms in Retail (45 minutes)**

**Objective:** Explore the tools that retail professionals use to harness AI for improving operations and customer engagement.

1. **Inventory & Supply Chain Optimization:**

   * **What AI Does:** Predicts demand, optimizes stock levels, automates replenishment, detects slow-moving or overstocked items.
   * **Example Tools:** Blue Yonder, Llamasoft (Coupa), Oracle Retail AI.
   * **Use Cases:** AI forecasts holiday demand, optimizes warehouse stock placement, detects supply chain disruptions.

2. **Personalized Marketing & Customer Engagement:**

   * **What AI Does:** Segments customers, generates personalized recommendations, automates marketing campaigns, optimizes dynamic pricing.
   * **Example Tools:** Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Dynamic Yield, Adobe Experience Cloud.
   * **Use Cases:** AI suggests products based on past purchases, triggers personalized promotions, adjusts pricing based on demand.

3. **Customer Service & Chatbots:**

   * **What AI Does:** Automates routine customer inquiries, provides product info, handles returns, detects sentiment, and escalates complex issues to humans.
   * **Example Tools:** Zendesk AI, LivePerson, Drift, Intercom.
   * **Use Cases:** AI-driven chatbots for FAQs, sentiment analysis for social media, and virtual assistants for e-commerce.

4. **Fraud Detection & Loss Prevention:**

   * **What AI Does:** Detects fraudulent transactions, identifies suspicious online activities, monitors in-store video for theft patterns, predicts high-risk suppliers.
   * **Example Tools:** Forter, Riskified, SAS Fraud Framework, Hikvision AI Security.
   * **Use Cases:** AI flags fraudulent credit card transactions, identifies fake returns, and detects theft using in-store cameras.

5. **Demand Forecasting & Sales Analytics:**

   * **What AI Does:** Predicts daily, weekly, and seasonal sales trends, identifies performance patterns, supports promotional strategies.
   * **Example Tools:** Microsoft Dynamics 365 AI, SAP Integrated Business Planning, Anaplan.
   * **Use Cases:** AI helps allocate inventory for promotions, predicts SKU performance, and analyzes sales across channels.

6. **Visual Merchandising & Store Layout Optimization:**

   * **What AI Does:** Analyzes shopper traffic, optimizes product placement, monitors planogram compliance, and detects congestion in aisles.
   * **Example Tools:** Trax Retail, RetailNext, Shelf Engine.
   * **Use Cases:** AI optimizes in-store displays and adjusts layouts based on foot traffic data.

---

#### **3. Hands-On Case Studies & Datasets (60 minutes)**

**Objective:** Allow learners to apply AI-driven strategies to real retail problems using sample datasets.

1. **Case Study 1 — Inventory Replenishment Optimization:**

   * **Scenario:** A chain of electronics stores struggles with stockouts for certain products and excess inventory for others.

   * **Sample Dataset Columns:**

     ```
     Store_ID | Product_ID | Daily_Sales | Current_Stock | Lead_Time_Days | Seasonality_Index | Reorder_Point
     ```

   * **Practice Tasks:**

     * Identify items at risk of stockouts.
     * Recommend reorder quantities based on AI predictions.
     * Simulate the impact of adjusting reorder points.

   * **Learning Outcomes:**

     * Understand how AI can optimize inventory levels.
     * Reduce the occurrence of overstocking and stockouts.
     * Improve customer satisfaction and store profitability.

2. **Case Study 2 — Personalized Marketing & Promotion:**

   * **Scenario:** An e-commerce brand wants to increase conversion rates with AI-driven product recommendations.

   * **Sample Dataset Columns:**

     ```
     Customer_ID | Age | Gender | Past_Purchases | Browsing_History | Engagement_Score | Promo_Response
     ```

   * **Practice Tasks:**

     * Segment customers for personalized campaigns.
     * Predict the next best product for each customer.
     * Evaluate campaign effectiveness based on past data.

   * **Learning Outcomes:**

     * Understand AI-driven customer segmentation.
     * Optimize marketing ROI with personalized campaigns.
     * Improve engagement and conversion rates.

3. **Case Study 3 — Store Traffic & Layout Optimization:**

   * **Scenario:** A fashion retail chain wants to optimize its in-store layout to improve sales.

   * **Sample Dataset Columns:**

     ```
     Store_ID | Product_Category | Foot_Traffic_Count | Dwell_Time | Conversion_Rate | Aisle_Location | Display_Effectiveness
     ```

   * **Practice Tasks:**

     * Analyze high-traffic and low-traffic zones.
     * Suggest layout changes to maximize sales and conversions.
     * Simulate the impact of product placement adjustments.

   * **Learning Outcomes:**

     * Optimize store layout based on AI insights.
     * Increase foot traffic and improve conversion rates.
     * Enhance the customer shopping experience.

#### **4. Group Assignments / Role-Playing Scenarios (45 minutes)**

**Objective:** Encourage learners to apply AI techniques in real-world retail scenarios through small-group activities.

1. **Assignment 1 — Inventory & Supply Chain Strategy:**

   * **Group Task:**

     1. Map out the current inventory replenishment process.
     2. Identify stockout or overstock issues.
     3. Propose an AI-enhanced replenishment strategy.
     4. Present a "Before → After" inventory plan.

   * **Role-Play Variation:**

     * Store Manager
     * Supply Chain Analyst
     * Procurement Specialist
     * AI Assistant (simulator)

2. **Assignment 2 — Personalized Marketing Campaign:**

   * **Group Task:**

     * Analyze the customer dataset.
     * Create AI-driven segmentation for personalized campaigns.
     * Design a promotional strategy and predict ROI.
     * Present expected customer engagement improvements.

   * **Role-Play Variation:**

     * Marketing Manager
     * Customer Experience Lead
     * AI Assistant (simulator)
     * Data Analyst

3. **Assignment 3 — Store Layout & Traffic Simulation:**

   * **Group Task:**

     * Use the foot traffic dataset to optimize store layout.
     * Identify areas for product placement changes.
     * Simulate the impact of layout changes on sales and conversion.

   * **Role-Play Variation:**

     * Visual Merchandiser
     * Store Manager
     * Customer
     * AI Traffic Simulation Assistant
