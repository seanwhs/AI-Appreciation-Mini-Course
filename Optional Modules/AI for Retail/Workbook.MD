# **2. AI in Retail ‚Äî The Big Picture**

Retail is highly AI-ready because of:

* Predicting customer behavior and preferences
* Large structured datasets (sales, inventory, CRM)
* Unstructured data analysis (reviews, social media, interactions)
* Optimizing supply chains and merchandising
* Repetitive operational workflows

**AI automates:** Inventory replenishment, sales forecasting, marketing personalization, fraud detection
**Humans handle:** Customer experience, merchandising strategy, supplier negotiation, complex decision-making
