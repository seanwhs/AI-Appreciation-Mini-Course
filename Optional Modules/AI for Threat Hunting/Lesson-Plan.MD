# **Lesson Plan: AI for Threat Hunting**

**Target Audience:** SOC analysts, threat hunters, security engineers, incident responders
**Duration:** 2–3 hours (adjustable)
**Delivery Mode:** Instructor-led, hybrid, or online

---

## **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:

1. Explain how AI integrates into threat hunting workflows.
2. Identify tasks in threat hunting suitable for AI augmentation or automation.
3. Use AI tools for log analysis, anomaly detection, and IOC correlation.
4. Apply AI to streamline hunting playbooks and proactive threat investigation.
5. Understand the limitations and human oversight requirements when using AI.

## **Learning Activities**

1. **Interactive Discussion:** Identify the most repetitive, alert-heavy, or time-consuming hunting tasks.
2. **Hands-On Demo:** AI-assisted log analysis and anomaly detection using Splunk or Sentinel dashboards.
3. **Group Workshop:** Threat intelligence enrichment and correlation with internal logs.
4. **Role-Play:** Analysts explore proactive hunting scenarios suggested by AI.
5. **AI Suitability Assessment:** Evaluate 5–7 threat-hunting tasks using the AI potential table.

---

## **Assessment & Evaluation**

* **Formative:** Group discussions, demos, and hands-on exercises.
* **Summative:** Multiple-choice or scenario-based quiz on AI application in threat hunting.
* **Practical Evaluation:** Completion of AI opportunity table and workflow improvement suggestions.

---

## **Materials & Resources**

* Slides / visual deck for AI in threat hunting
* Access to AI-enabled threat hunting platforms (Splunk ML Toolkit, Sentinel AI, Darktrace, Vectra)
* Collaboration tools (Miro, MURAL)
* Cheat sheet: “AI in Threat Hunting” one-page reference
* Sample telemetry and threat intelligence feeds for demos

---

## **Follow-Up & Reinforcement**

* Encourage participants to test AI-assisted tools in ongoing threat hunting exercises.
* Distribute the one-page AI cheat sheet for reference.
* Schedule follow-up sessions to review AI adoption impact and hunting efficiency gains.
