## **Section 3 ‚Äî Short Answer (5 Questions)**

1. Name two AI tools used for purple team detection engineering.
   **Sample Answer:** Splunk AI Assistant, Elastic Detections AI

2. How can AI help in mapping red-team actions to blue-team detections?
   **Sample Answer:** Correlates logs, tags actions with ATT&CK IDs, identifies which TTPs fired or were missed, highlights timing gaps

3. What are the benefits of AI-assisted coverage gap analysis?
   **Sample Answer:** Identifies blind spots, redundant detections, and rule tuning opportunities; prioritizes detection improvements

4. How does AI enhance purple-team collaboration?
   **Sample Answer:** Translates red actions for blue understanding, summarizes blue detections for red, generates shared dashboards, reduces misalignment

5. Why must humans remain involved in AI-driven purple team workflows?
   **Sample Answer:** To approve test scenarios, make strategic decisions, tune detections, and assess risk; AI augments but does not replace judgment
