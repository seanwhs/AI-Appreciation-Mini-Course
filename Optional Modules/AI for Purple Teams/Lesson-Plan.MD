# **Lesson Plan: AI for Purple Teams**

**Target Audience:** Red, blue, and purple team members; SOC analysts; threat intelligence teams
**Duration:** 2–3 hours (adjustable)
**Delivery Mode:** Instructor-led, hybrid, or online

---

## **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:

1. Explain how AI supports purple-team workflows.
2. Identify areas where AI can automate, augment, or accelerate purple-team tasks.
3. Use AI tools for scenario planning, detection validation, and gap analysis.
4. Apply AI insights to improve detection coverage, telemetry correlation, and continuous improvement.
5. Understand ethical boundaries and human oversight requirements for AI in purple teaming.

## **Learning Activities**

1. **Interactive Discussion:** Which purple-team tasks are most time-consuming or correlation-heavy?
2. **Hands-On Demo:** AI-assisted scenario generation and ATT&CK mapping using Caldera or PlexTrac.
3. **Group Workshop:** Correlate red actions with blue detections and identify gaps using AI dashboards.
4. **Role-Play:** Simulate an exercise where AI drafts after-action summaries and detection improvements.
5. **AI Suitability Assessment:** Evaluate 5–7 purple-team tasks using the AI potential table.

---

## **Assessment & Evaluation**

* **Formative:** Group discussions, demos, and hands-on exercises.
* **Summative:** Multiple-choice or scenario-based quiz on AI application in purple teaming.
* **Practical Evaluation:** Completion of AI opportunity table and workflow improvement suggestions.

---

## **Materials & Resources**

* Slides / visual deck of AI in purple teams
* Access to AI-enhanced purple-team platforms (Caldera, AttackIQ AI, PlexTrac)
* Collaboration tools (Miro, MURAL)
* Cheat sheet: “AI in Purple Teaming” one-page reference
* Sample telemetry and red-team logs for demonstrations

---

## **Follow-Up & Reinforcement**

* Encourage participants to test AI-assisted tools in their next purple-team exercise.
* Distribute the one-page AI cheat sheet for reference.
* Schedule follow-up sessions to review lessons learned and AI adoption impact.
