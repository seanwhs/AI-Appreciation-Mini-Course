# **2. What is Agentic AI?**

**Agentic AI** = AI that can **act autonomously** toward a goal without constant human input.

### **Key Characteristics**

* **Autonomy** ‚Äî acts without step-by-step human commands
* **Goal-Oriented** ‚Äî works toward a defined objective
* **Adaptability** ‚Äî learns from the environment and adjusts behavior
* **Decision-Making** ‚Äî evaluates actions and selects what best achieves its goal

# **3. How Agentic AI Works**

Agentic AI brings together several methods:

### **Reinforcement Learning**

Learns by trial and error:
‚úî Good actions ‚Üí rewards
‚úò Poor actions ‚Üí penalties

### **Planning & Decision-Making**

Uses models (e.g., MDPs) to:

* Predict outcomes
* Evaluate choices
* Select actions that move toward a goal

### **Perception**

Collects sensory data (camera, audio, lidar, system logs) to understand the environment.

### **Autonomous Action**

Uses what it perceives + what it knows to act with minimal oversight.

# **4. Applications of Agentic AI**

Below are major fields using Agentic AI.

### **Autonomous Vehicles**

Self-driving cars that navigate, avoid obstacles, and make route decisions.

### **Robotics**

Factory robots, warehouse sorters, household robots.

### **Finance**

Trading bots executing real-time trades.

### **Healthcare**

Personalized treatment planning, robotic surgery, autonomous patient monitoring.

### **Customer Service**

Virtual agents that answer questions, process orders, and troubleshoot.
