   **Sample Answer:** Autonomy, goal-orientation, adaptability, decision-making ability.

3. Give one real-world example of Agentic AI in robotics or automation.
   **Sample Answer:** Warehouse robots that sort and package items autonomously.

   **Sample Answer:** The AI learns through trial and error, receiving rewards for successful actions and penalties for mistakes, improving over time.

   **Sample Answer:** Bias in decision-making, accountability for errors, security vulnerabilities, human oversight.

6. How does an autonomous vehicle use Agentic AI to navigate safely?
   **Sample Answer:** It collects sensor data, identifies obstacles, predicts outcomes, and makes driving decisions independently.

7. Explain why human oversight is still important with Agentic AI.
   **Sample Answer:** Humans ensure safety, ethical compliance, and intervene if AI makes errors or unexpected decisions.

8. Provide one potential application of Agentic AI in finance and explain its benefit.
   **Sample Answer:** Algorithmic trading ‚Äî enables fast and adaptive decisions to maximize returns or manage risk autonomously.
