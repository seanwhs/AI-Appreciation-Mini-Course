# **3. Where AI Shows Up in Accounting Today**

---

## **A. Automated Data Entry & Document Processing**

**AI does:** Extracts data from invoices, PDFs, images; suggests GL codes; links transactions
**Use Cases:** Photo ‚Üí auto-filled invoice; batch import of vendor invoices; PDF ‚Üí clean spreadsheet
**Tools:** QuickBooks OCR, Dext, Hubdoc, Zoho Books Auto-Scan
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Excel ‚ÄúInsert Data from Picture,‚Äù Copilot cleanup, GPT for Sheets parsing

**Reflection:**
‚úèÔ∏è How much time could you save by automating document entry?

---

---

## **B. Bank Reconciliation & Transaction Matching**

**AI does:** Predicts categories, matches transactions, flags anomalies
**Use Cases:** Close books faster; auto-clear routine items; detect unusual withdrawals
**Tools:** QuickBooks Smart Matching, Xero Suggested Reconciliations
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Duet AI anomaly detection, GPT for Sheets matching

---

## **C. Accounts Payable (AP) Automation**

**AI does:** Routes invoices, suggests GL codes, detects duplicates, predicts cash impact
**Use Cases:** Flag duplicate invoices; identify suspicious vendors; recommend coding adjustments
**Tools:** Bill.com, Tipalti, Airbase
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot anomaly flags, Duet AI aging summaries

---

## **D. Accounts Receivable (AR) Automation**

**AI does:** Predicts late payments, personalizes reminders, prioritizes follow-ups
**Use Cases:** Risk scoring of invoices; automatic reminders; probabilistic cash forecasts
**Tools:** Chaser, Lockstep, Versapay
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot AR forecasts, GPT for Sheets reminders

---

## **E. Fraud Detection & Risk Monitoring**

**AI does:** Flags outliers, detects suspicious vendors, monitors transactions continuously
**Use Cases:** Reimbursements just below approval limits; backdated entries; unusual vendor changes
**Tools:** MindBridge, SAP Concur Detect, NetSuite Risk Intelligence
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot outlier detection, SheetAI anomaly scoring

---

## **F. Forecasting, Reporting & Financial Analysis**

**AI does:** Builds forecasts, identifies trends, explains variances, generates dashboards
**Use Cases:** Rolling cash forecasts; variance explanations in plain English; KPI insights
**Tools:** Fathom, Jirav, Datarails
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot variance analysis, Duet AI summaries

---

## **G. Tax Preparation & Compliance**

**AI does:** Classifies transactions, flags missing docs, applies tax rules
**Use Cases:** 1099 misclassifications, multi-state nexus, deduction flags
**Tools:** TurboTax AI, Avalara, Vertex
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot tax checks, GPT for Sheets classifications

---

## **H. Audit Support & Continuous Monitoring**

**AI does:** Scores transaction risk, detects anomalies, continuous monitoring, audit-ready summaries
**Use Cases:** Late-night postings, round-dollar entries, segregation-of-duties conflicts
**Tools:** MindBridge, CaseWare AnalyticsAI, Alteryx
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot formula validation, SheetAI risk scoring

---

## **I. Client Advisory Services (CAS)**

**AI does:** Summarizes financials, identifies risks & opportunities, generates visuals and narratives
**Use Cases:** Month-end client reports, benchmarking, margin insights
**Tools:** QuickBooks Business Insights, Fathom, Spotlight, ChatGPT/Copilot/Gemini
**Spreadsheet AI Examples:** Copilot narrative commentary, GPT for Sheets summaries
