# **AI for Accounting — Task Suitability Matrix**

| **Accounting Task / Activity**                 | **AI Potential** | **Notes / Examples**                                                            |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Data Entry & Document Processing               | High             | OCR for invoices, receipts, PDFs; GL coding suggestions; spreadsheet automation |
| Bank Reconciliation & Transaction Matching     | High             | Suggests matches, flags duplicates, predicts categories                         |
| Accounts Payable (AP) Processing               | High             | Duplicate detection, routing, GL recommendations, cash impact forecasting       |
| Accounts Receivable (AR) & Collections         | High             | Late payment prediction, automated reminders, cash flow forecasts               |
| Fraud Detection & Risk Monitoring              | High             | Anomaly detection, outlier identification, vendor risk monitoring               |
| Financial Reporting & Analysis (FP&A)          | Medium-High      | Dashboards, variance explanations, scenario analysis, trends identification     |
| Forecasting & Budgeting                        | Medium-High      | Rolling forecasts, predictive modeling, sensitivity analysis                    |
| Tax Preparation & Compliance                   | Medium           | Classification, missing document checks, jurisdiction rules, deduction flagging |
| Audit Support & Continuous Monitoring          | Medium-High      | Risk scoring, outlier detection, control checks, audit-ready summaries          |
| Client Advisory & Business Insights (CAS)      | Medium           | Narrative summaries, KPI benchmarking, talking points, recommendations          |
| Manual Spreadsheet Formula Creation            | Medium           | Copilot/Sheets AI can suggest formulas and correct errors                       |
| Professional Judgment / Strategic Decisions    | Low              | AI can assist, but human expertise is essential                                 |
| Regulatory Interpretation / Ethical Decisions  | Low              | High stakes, context-sensitive; AI cannot replace human judgment                |
| Client Communication & Relationship Management | Low              | AI may draft templates, but human personalization and empathy required          |

**Legend:**

* **High:** AI can largely automate or augment the task
* **Medium:** AI can assist, but human review/customization required
* **Low:** AI offers minimal support; human expertise essential

✅ **How to Use This Matrix in Training**

1. **Assessment Activity:** Learners list their daily accounting tasks, then map each task to **AI Potential** using the matrix.
2. **Tool Alignment:** Cross-reference tasks with recommended tools and spreadsheet AI examples to explore hands-on practice.
3. **Prioritization:** Focus AI implementation on **High AI Potential** tasks first for quick efficiency gains.
4. **Critical Thinking:** Discuss **Low AI Potential** tasks and why human judgment remains indispensable.
