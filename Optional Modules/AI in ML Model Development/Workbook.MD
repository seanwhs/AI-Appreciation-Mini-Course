### **Session 1: Introduction to AI and Machine Learning**

#### **1.1 Key Terms**

**Objective:** Familiarize yourself with the definitions of AI and ML.

**Instructions:** Match the following terms with their correct definitions.

1. **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**
2. **Machine Learning (ML)**
3. **Supervised Learning**
4. **Unsupervised Learning**
5. **Reinforcement Learning**

**Definitions:**

* A. Learning from labeled data, where the system makes predictions based on known input-output pairs.
* B. A system's ability to learn and adapt from its environment using feedback, often in the form of rewards or penalties.
* C. A branch of AI that allows machines to learn from data without explicit programming.
* D. The development of algorithms that mimic human intelligence.
* E. Learning from unlabeled data to find patterns or clusters in data.
