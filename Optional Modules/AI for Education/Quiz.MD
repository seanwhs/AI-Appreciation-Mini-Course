   **Sample Answer:** By scoring multiple-choice, short-answer, and essay assignments, providing feedback, and detecting plagiarism

   **Sample Answer:** To monitor student performance, predict at-risk learners, and recommend interventions

   **Sample Answer:** AI generates interactive simulations for science lessons or creates multiple versions of quizzes aligned with standards

## **Section 4 ‚Äî Application / Scenario (3 Questions)**

1. You notice AI predicts several students are at risk of failing. How should an educator respond?
   **Sample Answer:** Review AI predictions, validate with engagement and performance data, provide targeted interventions, and monitor outcomes

2. A teacher wants to use AI to personalize learning for a class. How can AI assist?
   **Sample Answer:** Recommend learning paths, adjust difficulty of lessons, provide targeted resources, and track mastery progress

3. During AI-assisted grading, the system flags potential plagiarism. What steps should follow?
   **Sample Answer:** Review flagged submissions, verify originality, provide feedback, and document findings for academic integrity
