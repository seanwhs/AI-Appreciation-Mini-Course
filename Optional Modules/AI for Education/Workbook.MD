# **3. Where AI Shows Up in Education**

### **1. Personalized Learning & Adaptive Instruction**

*Adjusts lessons based on student needs*

**Examples:** Knewton Alta, DreamBox Learning, Smart Sparrow, Century Tech

---

### **2. Automated Assessment & Grading**

*Grades assignments, provides feedback, detects plagiarism*

**Examples:** Gradescope, Turnitin, Querium, Edulastic

---

### **3. Learning Analytics & Student Performance Prediction**

*Predicts at-risk students, monitors engagement, supports interventions*

**Examples:** Civitas Learning, Brightspace Insights, Blackboard Analytics, Microsoft Education Insights

---

### **4. Intelligent Tutoring & Virtual Assistants**

*Answers questions, offers personalized tutoring, monitors progress*

**Examples:** Socratic by Google, Carnegie Learning, Watson Tutor, Quizlet AI

---

### **5. Curriculum Design & Content Generation**

*Generates lesson plans, quizzes, simulations, and adaptive curricula*

**Examples:** Canva for Education AI, OpenAI GPT tools, Chalkboard AI, TeachFX

---

### **6. Administrative Efficiency & Scheduling**

*Automates attendance, scheduling, notifications, and resource allocation*

**Examples:** Ellucian Banner, Microsoft Power Automate for Education, IBM Watson Education, Oracle Student Cloud

---

### **7. Agentic AI for Simulation & Scenario Planning**

*Models student progress, tests interventions, forecasts resource needs*

**Examples:** AnyLogic Education Simulation, EdTech Labs AI Analytics, MITRE Caldera AI (education adaptation)
