## **Section 1: Multiple Choice**

**1. Which task is AI best suited for in research paper writing?**
a) Developing a unique thesis statement
b) Summarizing key research articles
c) Interpreting experimental results
d) Making final ethical decisions about content

**Answer:** b) Summarizing key research articles

---

**2. AI can assist researchers by:**
a) Creating an outline for the paper
b) Drafting sections based on bullet points
c) Formatting citations and references
d) All of the above

**Answer:** d) All of the above

---

**3. Which of the following is NOT a common AI tool for academic writing?**
a) ChatGPT
b) Elicit.org
c) Zotero
d) Adobe Acrobat

**Answer:** d) Adobe Acrobat

---

**4. When should humans make the final decisions instead of AI?**
a) Summarizing articles
b) Choosing the most accurate and logical argument
c) Formatting references
d) Checking grammar

**Answer:** b) Choosing the most accurate and logical argument

---

**5. One advantage of using AI in research paper writing is:**
a) Guarantees originality without review
b) Speeds up research summarization and drafting
c) Eliminates the need for critical thinking
d) Automatically interprets results accurately

**Answer:** b) Speeds up research summarization and drafting
