## **Lesson Title:**

**Using AI to Streamline Research Paper Writing**

## **Duration:**

60‚Äì90 minutes

## **Audience:**

Students, researchers, or professionals who need to write academic or professional papers efficiently.

## **Lesson Goal:**

Learners will understand how to leverage AI to plan, draft, and refine research papers while maintaining academic integrity and critical thinking.

---

# **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this lesson, learners will be able to:

1. Identify AI tools for research, outlining, drafting, citation, and editing.
2. Use AI to generate paper outlines, summaries, drafts, and citations.
3. Critically evaluate and refine AI-generated content.
4. Integrate AI ethically into research writing, ensuring originality and proper citations.

---

# **Materials Needed**

* Computers or laptops with internet access
* Access to AI tools (ChatGPT, Elicit.org, Zotero, Grammarly, Turnitin)
* Sample research paper topics
* Worksheets for outlining, drafting, and citation practice

---

# **Lesson Outline**

---

## **1. Warm-Up Activity ‚Äî ‚ÄúYour Research Goal‚Äù (5‚Äì10 minutes)**

Ask learners to write:

* Their research topic
* The purpose of the paper (e.g., explore, argue, inform)
* Challenges they face in research or writing

**Purpose:** Connect AI tools to real writing challenges.

---

## **2. Introduction to AI in Research Writing (10 minutes)**

### **Talking Points:**

* Research papers involve topic selection, literature review, drafting, citing, and editing.
* AI can streamline repetitive and time-consuming tasks but does **not replace critical thinking**.
* Humans provide interpretation, argumentation, and ethical judgment.

**Discussion Prompt:**
*"Which part of writing a research paper is most time-consuming for you?"*

---

## **3. AI Applications in Research Paper Writing (25 minutes)**

### **1. Topic Selection & Research**

* AI suggests topics and summarizes relevant literature.
* **Demo:** ChatGPT generating research questions and key papers.

### **2. Outlining & Structuring**

* AI creates logical paper outlines (IMRaD or other formats).
* **Demo:** AI-generated outline for a 5-section paper.

### **3. Drafting & Content Generation**

* AI expands bullet points into paragraphs and drafts sections.
* **Demo:** ChatGPT drafting a literature review paragraph.

### **4. Citation & Reference Management**

* AI formats references in APA, MLA, or Chicago style.
* **Demo:** Zotero generating a bibliography from article DOIs.

### **5. Editing & Proofreading**

* AI checks grammar, clarity, and readability.
* **Demo:** Grammarly reviewing a sample paragraph.

### **6. Plagiarism & Originality Check**

* AI helps ensure proper citation and originality.
* **Demo:** Turnitin or Grammarly plagiarism check.

---

## **4. Hands-On Activity ‚Äî ‚ÄúDraft a Research Paper with AI‚Äù (20 minutes)**

1. Select a topic.
2. Use AI to:

   * Generate an outline
   * Summarize key sources
   * Draft introduction and one section
   * Generate references and citations
3. Review and refine AI content to ensure clarity, originality, and proper citations.

## **5. Ethics & Human Oversight (10 minutes)**

### **Key Points:**

* Verify AI-generated content for accuracy and bias.
* Ensure proper attribution and avoid plagiarism.
* Humans provide critical thinking, interpretation, and argumentation.

**Discussion Prompt:**
*"Which parts of a research paper should never rely solely on AI?"*

---

## **6. Wrap-Up & Key Takeaways (5 minutes)**

* AI can accelerate research, drafting, and formatting.
* Critical thinking, originality, and ethical judgment remain human responsibilities.
* Using AI effectively improves efficiency and allows more focus on analysis and insight.

---

## **Assessment / Exit Ticket**

1. Which stage of research paper writing will you use AI for?
2. Which stage will you handle personally and why?
