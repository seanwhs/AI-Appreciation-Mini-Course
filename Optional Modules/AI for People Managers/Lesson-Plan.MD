# **Lesson Plan: AI for People Managers**

## **Goal of This Lesson**

Equip people managers with the knowledge and skills to leverage AI tools for improving team performance, employee engagement, recruitment, and overall decision-making. Managers will learn how to partner with AI to make data-driven decisions while maintaining human-centered leadership.

---

## **Lesson Duration:** 90 minutes

---

## **Learning Objectives:**

By the end of this lesson, learners will be able to:

1. Identify key areas where AI can support people management (e.g., performance tracking, employee engagement, recruitment).
2. Explore AI tools that improve team dynamics, decision-making, and employee development.
3. Apply AI in optimizing workforce planning and improving employee retention.
4. Understand the balance between AI’s automation capabilities and the need for human leadership in people management.

---

## **Materials Needed:**

* Projector or screen for presentations
* Laptop or tablets (optional)
* Whiteboard and markers
* AI tools demo videos (optional)
* Case study materials (handouts or digital)

---

## **Lesson Outline:**

### **1. Introduction to AI for People Management (15 minutes)**

**Activity: Group Discussion**

* Ask participants: "What are some of the most time-consuming tasks you face in managing teams?"
* Introduce the concept of AI in people management, explaining its role in streamlining administrative work, improving decision-making, and enhancing team performance.

**Key Points to Cover:**

* AI in people management automates routine tasks like scheduling, performance reviews, and employee engagement analysis.
* AI supports data-driven insights for better decision-making without replacing human judgment, emotional intelligence, and strategic thinking.

---

### **2. AI Tools for People Management (30 minutes)**

**Section Overview:**

* Overview of AI tools that support different aspects of people management.
* Show how AI can be used to optimize employee engagement, performance management, recruitment, and retention.

#### **AI for Employee Engagement:**

* **Tools:** Peakon (Workday), Officevibe, CultureAmp
* **How AI Helps:** Monitors engagement through real-time feedback, identifies at-risk employees, and provides actionable insights for improving morale and satisfaction.

#### **AI for Performance Management:**

* **Tools:** 15Five, BetterWorks, Lattice
* **How AI Helps:** Tracks employee performance metrics, helps set and align goals, and provides continuous feedback to improve individual and team performance.

#### **AI for Recruitment:**

* **Tools:** HireVue, X0PA AI, Pymetrics
* **How AI Helps:** Screens candidates faster, reduces unconscious bias in hiring, and identifies the best-fit candidates based on data.

#### **AI for Workforce Planning & Retention:**

* **Tools:** Visier, Workday, ADP DataCloud
* **How AI Helps:** Predicts workforce trends, analyzes turnover rates, and suggests strategies to retain high-performing employees.

**Demo / Video (Optional):**

* Show a short demo or video on AI-powered tools for people managers. You can use platform demos or video walkthroughs.

---

### **3. Breakout Activity: Identifying AI Opportunities (20 minutes)**

**Instructions:**

* Divide learners into small groups (3–4 participants).
* Each group selects one of the following scenarios to analyze:

  1. **Employee Engagement** – How can AI help improve employee satisfaction and retention in your team?
  2. **Performance Management** – How can AI-driven performance metrics and feedback systems improve your team's efficiency and outcomes?
  3. **Recruitment** – How can AI streamline recruitment and reduce bias in hiring decisions?
  4. **Workforce Planning** – How can AI help forecast team growth, manage turnover, and improve talent allocation?

**Outcome:**

* Each group should brainstorm and outline key AI tools that could support their scenario and present to the larger group.

**Key Deliverables:**

* AI tools that fit the scenario.
* Expected outcomes (improvements in engagement, performance, recruitment efficiency, etc.).
* Potential challenges and solutions.

---

### **4. Case Study: AI in Recruitment & Talent Development (15 minutes)**

**Case Study Overview:**

* Share a case study or scenario involving a company struggling with high turnover and inefficient recruitment. The company decides to implement AI tools to streamline the hiring process, predict turnover, and support employee growth.
* Example: A mid-size company using AI to predict turnover risk by analyzing engagement surveys, performance data, and career development progress.

**Discussion Questions:**

* How could AI help reduce turnover in this scenario?
* What role would a people manager play in this process?
* What are some potential challenges to AI adoption in recruitment and development?

**Debrief:**

* Discuss insights and potential hurdles managers might face when introducing AI in recruitment, engagement, and performance management.

---

### **5. Key Takeaways and Closing Discussion (10 minutes)**

**Discussion:**

* Recap the main AI tools discussed and how they fit into different aspects of people management.
* Engage the group in a final discussion:

  * How can AI support **your specific role** as a people manager?
  * What challenges do you foresee in integrating AI tools into your existing processes?
  * How can AI be used in a way that complements human judgment?

**Closing Remarks:**

* Reinforce that AI is a tool for enhancing the work of people managers, not replacing the human element of leadership.
* Encourage managers to experiment with AI tools, evaluate their effectiveness, and consider how they can integrate AI into their workflows to save time and improve outcomes.

---

## **Assessment (Optional)**

At the end of the lesson, learners can complete a **short quiz** or **self-reflection exercise** that includes:

1. **Multiple-Choice Questions** (e.g., "Which AI tool helps automate performance reviews?")
2. **Short Answer** (e.g., "Describe a scenario where AI could improve employee engagement in your team.")
3. **AI Integration Plan**: Ask participants to outline a short plan for how they could integrate AI into their current people management practices.

---

## **Lesson Summary:**

* **AI can augment people managers** by automating administrative tasks, providing data-driven insights, and improving employee engagement, recruitment, and performance management.
* **AI tools** that focus on engagement, performance, recruitment, and retention offer real-time, actionable insights that can improve team outcomes and organizational health.
* **Human leadership** remains essential in people management, with AI providing support for tasks that require data analysis, trend identification, and efficiency improvements.

**Takeaway for Learners:**

* AI is here to complement and enhance the work of people managers by reducing time spent on routine tasks and giving more space for leadership, coaching, and strategic decision-making.
