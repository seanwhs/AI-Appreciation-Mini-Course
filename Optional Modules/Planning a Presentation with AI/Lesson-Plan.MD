## **Lesson Title:**

**Enhancing Presentations with AI Tools**

## **Duration:**

## **Audience:**

Students, professionals, educators, or anyone looking to improve presentation creation with AI.

## **Lesson Goal:**

Learners will understand how to use AI to plan, structure, design, and optimize presentations efficiently while maintaining creativity and audience engagement.

---

# **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this lesson, learners will be able to:

1. Identify AI tools for presentation planning, design, and content generation.
2. Use AI to create slide outlines, visuals, and speaker notes.
3. Evaluate and refine AI-generated content for clarity, engagement, and style.
4. Apply AI tools ethically and effectively, combining AI assistance with human creativity.

---

## **2. Introduction to AI in Presentation Planning (10 minutes)**

### **Talking Points:**

* Presentation planning requires research, structuring, visuals, and speaking preparation.
* AI can automate repetitive tasks, summarize research, generate visuals, and suggest slide content.
* Humans provide creativity, storytelling, and personalization.

**Discussion Prompt:**
*"Which part of creating presentations do you find most time-consuming or challenging?"*

---

## **3. AI Applications in Presentation Planning (20 minutes)**

### **1. Topic Research & Content Generation**

* AI summarizes research and generates slide content.
* **Demo:** ChatGPT creating bullet points for a slide topic.

### **2. Slide Structure & Outlining**

* AI suggests logical flow and slide order.
* **Demo:** Beautiful.ai or Tome generating a 5-slide outline.

### **3. Visual Design & Data Representation**

* AI designs charts, infographics, and layouts.
* **Demo:** Canva AI creating a chart from sample data.

### **4. Speaking Notes & Script Assistance**

* AI drafts concise talking points and prompts for engagement.
* **Demo:** ChatGPT suggesting speaker notes for each slide.

### **5. Review & Optimization**

* AI can proofread, check clarity, and recommend simplifications.
* **Demo:** Grammarly or Copilot reviewing slides.

---

## **5. Ethics, Human Oversight & Personalization (10 minutes)**

### **Key Points:**

* Verify AI content for accuracy, tone, and appropriateness.
* Tailor AI-generated slides to match audience, context, and style.
* Humans ensure storytelling, engagement, and ethical considerations.

**Discussion Prompt:**
*"Which parts of a presentation should never rely solely on AI?"*

---

## **6. Wrap-Up & Key Takeaways (5 minutes)**

* AI can **accelerate research, content creation, and visual design**.
* Human creativity and judgment remain critical for engagement and personalization.
* Using AI effectively results in **efficient, professional, and impactful presentations**.
