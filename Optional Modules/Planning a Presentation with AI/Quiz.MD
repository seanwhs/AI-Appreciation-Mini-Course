## **Section 1: Multiple Choice**

**1. Which task is AI best suited for in presentation planning?**
a) Crafting a personal anecdote to engage the audience
b) Designing charts and visuals based on data
c) Choosing the audience-specific tone for a sensitive topic
d) Making final ethical decisions about content

**Answer:** b) Designing charts and visuals based on data

---

**2. AI can assist presenters by:**
a) Generating slide outlines
b) Summarizing research for key points
c) Drafting speaker notes and prompts
d) All of the above

**Answer:** d) All of the above

---

**3. Which of the following is NOT a common AI tool for presentations?**
a) ChatGPT
b) Canva AI
c) Beautiful.ai
d) Adobe Acrobat

**Answer:** d) Adobe Acrobat

---

**4. When should humans make the final presentation decision instead of AI?**
a) Choosing slide layouts
b) Adding a personal story for engagement
c) Creating a bar chart from data
d) Generating bullet points

**Answer:** b) Adding a personal story for engagement

---

**5. One advantage of using AI in presentation planning is:**
a) Guarantees audience engagement
b) Saves time on research, content, and design
c) Replaces human creativity
d) Eliminates the need to review content

**Answer:** b) Saves time on research, content, and design
