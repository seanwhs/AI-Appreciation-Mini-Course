### **Workbook for "Innovating Together – The Art and Science of Human-AI Collaboration"**

---

#### **Module Overview**

This workbook will help you reflect on the content of each section of the course, actively apply the concepts, and guide you through exercises that help you integrate AI collaboration into your work, life, and play.

By completing the activities in this workbook, you will:

1. **Understand AI’s role** in work, life, and play.
2. **Identify AI-accelerated and AI-resistant tasks**.
3. **Spot new opportunities and roles** created by AI.
4. **Cultivate the core skills** needed to thrive in an AI-infused world.
5. **Design your AI-powered future** and become an AI collaborator.

---
