# **AI in HelpDesk ‚Äî The Big Picture**

Helpdesk teams manage tasks such as resolving technical issues, responding to tickets, assisting users with software or hardware, and maintaining documentation. Many tasks are:

* **Repetitive** (password resets, common troubleshooting)
* **Information-heavy** (knowledge base searches, system status checks)
* **Time-sensitive** (urgent tickets, SLA compliance)
* **Decision-making intensive** (diagnosing issues, escalation)

AI can reduce workload, speed up responses, and allow human agents to focus on complex problem-solving and user interaction.

### **AI automates:**

* Ticket classification and routing
* FAQs and routine troubleshooting
* System monitoring and alerts
* Knowledge base suggestions
* Summarizing tickets and chat transcripts
* Predictive issue detection

### **Humans handle:**

* Complex problem-solving and critical decisions
* Escalations and system-wide incident response
* Communication requiring empathy or negotiation
* Policy compliance and security-sensitive decisions

# **1. Ticket Classification & Prioritization**

AI helps categorize incoming tickets and assign urgency for faster resolution.

### **What AI Does**

* Automatically classifies tickets by issue type
* Determines priority based on keywords, user role, and system impact
* Routes tickets to the appropriate team or agent

### **Use Cases**

* Auto-tagging network, software, or hardware issues
* Escalating high-impact tickets immediately
* Reducing manual sorting time

### **Real Tools**

ServiceNow, Zendesk AI, Freshservice AI, Jira Service Management

### **Platform Examples**

* ServiceNow AI for ticket classification
* Jira Service Management AI routing suggestions

---

# **2. Knowledge Base & Troubleshooting Support**

AI assists agents and users in finding the right solutions quickly.

### **What AI Does**

* Suggests articles or steps from knowledge base
* Updates knowledge base automatically from resolved tickets
* Generates troubleshooting scripts

### **Use Cases**

* Quickly provide solution steps to common errors
* Enable self-service for end users
* Reduce repetitive agent queries

### **Real Tools**

Guru, Confluence AI, ServiceNow Knowledge, Zendesk Guide

### **Platform Examples**

* AI-driven suggestions in ServiceNow tickets
* Auto-suggest knowledge articles in Zendesk

### **Real Tools**

### **Platform Examples**

AI summarizes ticket content for faster review and reporting.

AI helps proactively detect issues before users report them.

# üßæ **One-Page Cheat Sheet ‚Äî ‚ÄúAI for HelpDesk‚Äù**

### **AI Excels At:**

* Ticket classification and prioritization
* Knowledge base suggestions
* Automated responses and chatbots
* Summarization and reporting
* Predictive alerts and monitoring

### **When NOT to Use AI:**

* Handling complex or sensitive incidents
* Escalations requiring critical thinking
* Situations involving high-risk security or compliance issues
* Tasks requiring human empathy or negotiation

### **Bottom Line:**

AI **supports helpdesk professionals** in routine, repetitive, or analytical tasks, allowing them to **focus on problem-solving, incident resolution, and user experience**.
