### **Explanation of Correct Answers:**

1. AI's main benefit in TTX is making the scenarios more adaptive and engaging by dynamically adjusting them based on team actions.
2. AI is excellent at automating tasks like scenario generation, while human teams focus on decision-making.
3. AI creates attack scenarios based on up-to-date threat intelligence, ensuring that they are realistic and challenging.
