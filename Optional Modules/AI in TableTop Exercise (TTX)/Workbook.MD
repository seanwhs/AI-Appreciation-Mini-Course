# **AI in Tabletop Exercises (TTX) Workbook**

## **Course Overview:**

This workbook is designed to support the learning process for **AI in Tabletop Exercises (TTX)**. It includes activities, exercises, and reflective questions that will help reinforce the concepts taught during the lesson, provide hands-on experience with AI tools, and encourage critical thinking on how to integrate AI into your tabletop exercises for improved incident response.

### **2. How AI Enhances TTX**

**Activity 2: AI Benefits in TTX**

Read the following sections on how AI can enhance different aspects of TTX (Scenario Generation, Real-Time Injects, Documentation, etc.). After each section, reflect on the following questions:

* **Scenario Generation & Simulation:**

  * How can AI’s ability to simulate adaptive adversary behavior improve incident response training?
  * How might AI-generated scenarios be different from traditional TTXs?

---

* **Real-Time Injects & Adaptive Attack Simulations:**

  * What are the advantages of having AI introduce evolving attack techniques during a TTX?
  * How can real-time injects help in assessing the responsiveness of different teams (e.g., security, legal, PR)?

---

* **Incident Documentation & Reporting:**

  * How does automating incident documentation benefit the facilitator and the participants?
  * In what ways can AI’s automatic reporting assist in post-incident analysis and learning?

### **4. Hands-On Activity: AI in Action**

**Activity 6: Scenario Simulation**

In the hands-on portion of the lesson, you used AI to simulate an evolving cyberattack scenario. Reflect on the following:

1. **Scenario:** What was the nature of the cyberattack simulated during the exercise (e.g., phishing attack, ransomware)?
2. **AI Role:** How did the AI tool adapt the scenario in real-time based on your team’s actions?
3. **Response:** How did your team’s decisions change as the scenario evolved due to AI-driven injects?
4. **Lessons Learned:** What did you learn about your team’s communication, response time, and decision-making? How can AI help in identifying areas of improvement?
