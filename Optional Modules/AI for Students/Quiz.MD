## **Section 3 ‚Äî Short Answer (5 Questions)**

1. Name two AI tools for note-taking and summarization.
   **Sample Answer:** Notion AI, Otter.ai

2. How can AI assist in research literature review?
   **Sample Answer:** AI can search for relevant sources, summarize key points, extract citations, and suggest related topics.

3. Give one example of AI supporting STEM problem solving.
   **Sample Answer:** Wolfram Alpha providing step-by-step solutions and visualizing graphs.

4. What is one benefit of AI-powered study scheduling tools?
   **Sample Answer:** They create optimized study plans, prioritize tasks, and adjust schedules dynamically.

5. When should a student avoid relying on AI?
   **Sample Answer:** When tasks require critical thinking, original ideas, or ethical judgment.

## **Section 4 ‚Äî Scenario / Application (3 Questions)**

1. You have three exams in one week. How can AI help you prioritize and schedule study sessions?
   **Sample Answer:** AI can generate a study timetable based on subject difficulty, integrate spaced repetition, include breaks, and adjust for missed sessions.

2. You need to write a research essay. How would you leverage AI responsibly?
   **Sample Answer:** Use AI to summarize articles, suggest outlines, check citations, and draft portions while ensuring personal critical analysis and interpretation.

3. You are working on a group project with mixed schedules. How can AI assist in collaboration?
   **Sample Answer:** AI can summarize meeting notes, suggest slide content, manage shared documents, and propose workflow optimizations.
