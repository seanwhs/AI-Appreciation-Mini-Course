# **3. How AI Helps Seniors**

---

## **A. Voice Assistants**

**What AI Does:** Recognizes commands, answers questions, controls devices.
**Benefits:** Hands-free operation, easier access to information, control of smart devices.
**Examples:** Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri

**Try It Yourself:**

1. Ask the assistant: "What's the weather today?"
2. Set a reminder: "Remind me to take my medicine at 3 PM."
3. Play music or control lights: "Turn on the kitchen light."

**Reflection:**

## **B. Healthcare & Wellness**

**What AI Does:** Tracks health, provides virtual consultations, sends medication reminders.
**Benefits:** Supports healthy habits, monitors chronic conditions, improves access to care.
**Examples:** Fitbit, Apple Health, Teladoc, Health Mate

**Try It Yourself:**

1. Wear a smartwatch or fitness tracker and check your steps or heart rate.
