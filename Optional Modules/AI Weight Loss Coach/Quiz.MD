## **Section 1: Multiple Choice**

**1. What is a primary advantage of using AI for weight loss?**
a) AI can physically exercise for you
b) AI can provide personalized meal and workout plans efficiently
c) AI replaces all human decision-making
d) AI can guarantee rapid weight loss without effort

**Answer:** b) AI can provide personalized meal and workout plans efficiently

---

**2. Which task still requires human judgment even when using AI?**
a) Logging meals automatically
b) Adjusting meal flavors or portion sizes to taste
c) Tracking steps or workouts
d) Generating a grocery list

**Answer:** b) Adjusting meal flavors or portion sizes to taste

---

**3. AI can help with which of the following?**
a) Calculating calories and macros
b) Suggesting substitutions for dietary restrictions
c) Monitoring progress and trends over time
d) All of the above

**Answer:** d) All of the above

---

**4. Which of these is NOT an AI tool commonly used in weight loss?**
a) MyFitnessPal
b) Noom
c) Fitbit
d) Photoshop

**Answer:** d) Photoshop

---

**5. Motivation and habit tracking with AI helps primarily by:**
a) Replacing the need for personal effort
b) Sending reminders, encouragement, and tracking adherence
c) Eliminating workouts
d) Automatically losing weight without lifestyle changes

**Answer:** b) Sending reminders, encouragement, and tracking adherence
