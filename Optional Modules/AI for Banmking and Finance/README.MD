# **AI in Banking & Finance — The Big Picture**

Banking and finance are highly data-driven, making them AI-ready because much of the work depends on:

* Large structured datasets (transactions, accounts, loans)
* Unstructured data (news, social media, call center transcripts)
* Risk analysis, fraud detection, and regulatory compliance
* Predictive analytics for lending, investments, and customer behavior
* Repetition in operational workflows

AI excels in these environments and reduces manual workload while enhancing precision.
But **AI does not replace finance professionals**—it shifts them from routine calculations and monitoring to decision-making, strategy, and client advisory roles.

### **AI automates:**

Repetitive, high-volume, rules-based tasks such as transaction monitoring, credit scoring, and reporting.

### **Humans handle:**

Strategic financial decisions, customer relationship management, compliance interpretation, and ethical oversight.

Most modern banking systems, trading platforms, and fintech applications already embed AI—sometimes transparently, sometimes behind the scenes.

# **1. Fraud Detection & Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**

AI protects assets and ensures compliance.

### **What AI Does**

* Monitors transactions in real time for unusual patterns
* Flags potential fraudulent activity or money laundering
* Reduces false positives in alerts
* Analyzes customer behavior for anomalies

### **Use Cases**

* Detect suspicious wire transfers
* Flag unusual card activity or account logins
* Analyze network of transactions for AML compliance

### **Real Tools**

Darktrace • SAS Fraud Framework • FICO Falcon • Featurespace ARIC

---

# **2. Credit Scoring & Risk Assessment**

AI improves lending decisions and portfolio management.

### **What AI Does**

* Analyzes creditworthiness beyond traditional FICO scores
* Predicts default risk and delinquency probability
* Monitors portfolio risk dynamically
* Suggests optimal loan terms

### **Use Cases**

* Predict borrower likelihood of default
* Evaluate small business loan applications
* Optimize credit line adjustments

### **Real Tools**

Zest AI • Upstart • Experian AI Credit Scoring • FICO Analytics

---

# **3. Algorithmic Trading & Investment Management**

AI augments traders and portfolio managers.

### **What AI Does**

* Analyzes market data and news sentiment
* Generates trading signals based on predictive models
* Optimizes portfolio allocations
* Supports robo-advisory services

### **Use Cases**

* High-frequency trading with AI-optimized strategies
* Predict asset price movements
* Auto-rebalance client portfolios based on risk appetite

### **Real Tools**

Kavout • QuantConnect • Alpaca AI • BlackRock Aladdin

---

# **4. Customer Service & Virtual Assistants**

AI improves client engagement and operational efficiency.

### **What AI Does**

* Handles routine inquiries via chat or voice
* Provides account information, transaction history, or product guidance
* Detects sentiment and escalates complex cases to human agents

### **Use Cases**

* AI-powered chatbots for banking apps
* Virtual assistants for investment advice
* Call center automation with AI transcription and analysis

### **Real Tools**

Kasisto KAI • Nuance Virtual Assistants • Intercom AI • IBM Watson Assistant

---

# **5. Regulatory Compliance & Reporting**

AI ensures adherence to complex financial regulations.

### **What AI Does**

* Monitors transactions and operations for compliance breaches
* Generates regulatory reports automatically
* Tracks evolving legislation and policy changes
* Detects anomalies requiring review

### **Use Cases**

* Automate AML and KYC compliance checks
* Generate Basel III / IFRS 9 risk reports
* Monitor trading compliance in investment banks

### **Real Tools**

Ayasdi AML • ComplyAdvantage • Workiva AI • Ascent RegTech

---

# **6. Predictive Analytics & Financial Forecasting**

AI supports strategic planning and operations.

### **What AI Does**

* Forecasts cash flows, revenues, and market trends
* Detects early signals of credit risk or operational anomalies
* Identifies opportunities for investment or cost reduction

### **Use Cases**

* Predict loan default rates for portfolio management
* Forecast FX or interest rate trends
* Anticipate liquidity needs for banks or corporates

### **Real Tools**

IBM Watson Financial Services • SAS Analytics • Alteryx • FICO Analytic Cloud

---

# **7. Agentic AI for Simulation & Scenario Testing**

AI simulates financial operations, market conditions, and stress scenarios.

### **What Agentic AI Does**

* Generates realistic market or transaction scenarios
* Tests portfolio resilience under volatility
* Simulates operational or regulatory stress tests
* Suggests mitigation strategies before actual implementation

### **Use Cases**

* Stress-test bank capital adequacy under crisis scenarios
* Generate synthetic transactions for fraud model training
* Test impact of new trading strategies or policies

### **Real Tools**

AnyLogic Financial Simulation • MITRE Caldera AI (finance adaptation) • Numerix Scenario Simulator

# **Activity — “Where Can AI Strengthen Your Banking Practice?”**

Learners list 5–7 banking or finance tasks and evaluate them using AI suitability criteria:

* High-volume transactions or data
* Repetitive, rules-based processes
* Risk of human error
* Predictive or real-time needs
* Regulatory or compliance-sensitive
* Customer interaction heavy

Tasks meeting **2+ criteria** are strong candidates for AI augmentation, including agentic AI for scenario simulation.
