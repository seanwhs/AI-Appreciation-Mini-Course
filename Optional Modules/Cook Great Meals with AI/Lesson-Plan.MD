## **Lesson Title:**

**Using AI to Plan, Prepare, and Enhance Meals**

## **Duration:**

60‚Äì90 minutes

## **Audience:**

Home cooks, culinary students, or anyone interested in using AI to improve meal planning and cooking efficiency.

## **Lesson Goal:**

Learners will understand how to use AI tools to plan meals, create recipes, manage ingredients, and improve nutrition while maintaining creativity and personal taste in cooking.

---

# **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this lesson, learners will be able to:

1. Identify AI tools for meal planning, recipe creation, and ingredient management.
2. Use AI to generate recipes, shopping lists, and dietary suggestions.
3. Evaluate AI recommendations for taste, creativity, and nutrition.
4. Integrate AI into cooking routines responsibly to save time and enhance meal quality.

---

# **Materials Needed**

* Devices with internet access (computer, tablet, or smartphone)
* Access to AI tools (ChatGPT, Yummly, Mealime, Whisk, SideChef, MyFitnessPal)
* Sample ingredients, recipes, or meal ideas
* Worksheet for planning meals and ingredients

---

# **Lesson Outline**

---

## **1. Warm-Up Activity ‚Äî ‚ÄúYour Cooking Challenges‚Äù (5‚Äì10 minutes)**

Ask learners to write down:

* Meals they cook regularly
* Challenges they face in planning or preparing meals
* Tasks they wish could be simplified or optimized

**Purpose:** Connect AI applications to real cooking challenges.

---

## **2. Introduction to AI in Cooking (10 minutes)**

### **Talking Points:**

* Cooking involves planning, prepping, and cooking efficiently while considering nutrition and taste.
* AI can streamline repetitive tasks, suggest recipes, and provide dietary guidance.
* Human creativity, flavor judgment, and ethical choices remain essential.

**Discussion Prompt:**
*"Which part of your cooking process could benefit most from AI assistance?"*

---

## **3. AI Applications in Cooking (25 minutes)**

### **1. Meal Planning & Recipe Creation**

* AI generates weekly meal plans or new recipes based on preferences or available ingredients.
* **Demo:** ChatGPT creating a week‚Äôs worth of vegan dinners.

### **2. Grocery Shopping & Ingredient Management**

* AI creates shopping lists, tracks pantry items, and suggests substitutions.
* **Demo:** Whisk generating a grocery list from planned recipes.

### **3. Cooking Assistance & Technique Guidance**

* AI provides step-by-step instructions, timers, and tips for techniques.
* **Demo:** SideChef guiding through a complex dish with timers.

### **4. Nutrition & Dietary Assistance**

* AI calculates nutrition, tracks macros, and suggests alternatives for dietary restrictions.
* **Demo:** MyFitnessPal assessing the nutrition of AI-generated recipes.

## **5. Ethics & Human Oversight (10 minutes)**

### **Key Points:**

* Verify AI-generated recipes for accuracy and safety.
* Taste, creativity, and personal preferences are irreplaceable.
* Use AI responsibly as a support tool, not a replacement for cooking skills.

**Discussion Prompt:**
*"Which aspects of cooking should always rely on your own judgment rather than AI?"*

---

## **6. Wrap-Up & Key Takeaways (5 minutes)**

* AI can enhance efficiency, inspiration, and nutrition planning in cooking.
* Human creativity, flavor judgment, and dietary decision-making remain crucial.
* Partnering with AI allows for more organized, enjoyable, and nutritious meals.

---

## **Assessment / Exit Ticket**

1. Which cooking task would you use AI for, and why?
2. Which task still requires your personal judgment or taste?
3. How will you integrate AI into your cooking responsibly?

---

## **Homework / Optional Practice**

* Plan one full week of meals using AI.
* Generate a shopping list and identify ingredient substitutions.
* Try cooking a recipe entirely with AI guidance, then evaluate taste, efficiency, and learning outcomes.

---

This lesson plan sets the stage for **practical, hands-on learning with AI in cooking** while emphasizing creativity, safety, and taste.
