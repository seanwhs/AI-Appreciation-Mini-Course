**1. What is the main advantage of using AI in meal planning?**
a) AI can taste the food for you
b) AI can generate recipes and create grocery lists efficiently
c) AI can cook the meals automatically
d) AI replaces creativity in cooking

**Answer:** b) AI can generate recipes and create grocery lists efficiently

---

**2. Which task still requires human judgment even when using AI?**
a) Organizing a grocery list
b) Adjusting flavors and seasoning to taste
c) Generating weekly meal plans
d) Tracking pantry inventory

**Answer:** b) Adjusting flavors and seasoning to taste

---

**3. AI can assist with which of the following dietary tasks?**
a) Calculating nutritional values
b) Suggesting ingredient substitutions for allergies
c) Planning meals according to specific diets
d) All of the above

**Answer:** d) All of the above

---

**4. Which of these is NOT an AI tool commonly used in cooking?**
a) Yummly
b) ChatGPT
c) SideChef
d) Adobe Illustrator

**Answer:** d) Adobe Illustrator

**5. A benefit of AI-assisted cooking is:**
a) It guarantees perfect taste without human input
b) It helps save time on planning, shopping, and tracking nutrition

**Answer:** b) It helps save time on planning, shopping, and tracking nutrition
