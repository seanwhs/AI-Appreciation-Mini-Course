# **2. AI in Healthcare ‚Äî The Big Picture**

Healthcare is AI-ready because of:

* Pattern recognition (imaging, lab results)
* Large structured/unstructured datasets (EHRs, claims)
* Risk stratification and predictive modeling
* Documentation and coding
* Repetitive administrative workflows

**AI automates:** Documentation, coding, scheduling, triage, billing, and claims review
**Humans handle:** Clinical judgment, patient communication, complex diagnostics, care planning, ethical decisions
