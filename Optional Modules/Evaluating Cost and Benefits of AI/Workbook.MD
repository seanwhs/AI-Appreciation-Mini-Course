## **4. Cost-Benefit Analysis Framework**

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Select a **specific AI tool or scenario**
2. List **all costs** (financial, human, opportunity, ethical/social)
3. List **all benefits** (quantitative and qualitative)
4. **Quantify** costs and benefits wherever possible
5. Calculate **Net Benefit**:

[
\text{Net Benefit} = \text{Total Benefits} - \text{Total Costs}
]

6. Decide if AI adoption is **cost-justifiable**

**Facilitator Tip:** Use a matrix template with **quantitative and qualitative columns** for discussion.
