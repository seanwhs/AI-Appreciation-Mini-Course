### **Section 4: Automating Tasks with AI**

**4.1 Using Macros to Automate Tasks**
**Macros** allow you to automate repetitive tasks by recording your actions in Excel.

**Activity 4: Recording a Simple Macro**

1. In Excel, go to **View > Macros > Record Macro**.
2. Perform a series of steps (e.g., format data, sort rows).
3. Stop recording and save the macro.
4. Re-run the macro to automate the task.

### **Section 6: Best Practices for Integrating AI into Your Excel Workflow**

**6.1 Tips for Using AI in Excel**

* **Keep Data Clean**: AI performs better when the data is well-organized and free from errors.
* **Start Small**: Begin by using simple features like **Ideas** and **Forecast Sheet** to get familiar with AI in Excel.
* **Combine AI with Traditional Tools**: Enhance your work by combining AI tools with Excel functions like pivot tables and charts.
* **Experiment**: Explore add-ins and more advanced AI tools as you grow comfortable with Excel's built-in features.
