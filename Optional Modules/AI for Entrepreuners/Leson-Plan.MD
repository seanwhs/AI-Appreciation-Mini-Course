# **Lesson Plan — AI for Entrepreneurs**

**Duration:** 3–4 hours (flexible for half-day workshop)
**Audience:** Aspiring and early-stage entrepreneurs, small business owners, startup teams

---

## **Module Goal**

Equip learners to:

* Recognize where AI appears in entrepreneurial workflows
* Identify AI tools and platforms for business tasks
* Understand what tasks AI can automate, augment, or accelerate
* Partner with AI to improve efficiency, research, operations, and innovation

---

## **Lesson Objectives**

By the end of the session, learners will be able to:

1. Describe AI applications in market research, marketing, finance, product development, and workflow automation.
2. Evaluate business tasks for AI suitability using practical criteria.
3. Use AI tools to generate insights, content, and financial analyses.
4. Distinguish tasks requiring human judgment from those AI can optimize.

---

## **Lesson Structure**

| Segment                                   | Duration | Activities                                                                                          | Learning Outcome                                                                  |
| ----------------------------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Introduction & Big Picture**            | 15 min   | Lecture + discussion: AI in entrepreneurship, repetitive vs. strategic tasks                        | Learners understand why AI is a productivity accelerator, not a replacement       |
| **AI in Market Research**                 | 30 min   | Demo: Crayon, GrowthBar, ChatGPT                                                                    | Learners see AI summarizing competitors, trends, and SWOT                         |
| **Content Creation & Marketing**          | 30 min   | Group activity: Generate social media posts/email campaigns with Jasper AI                          | Learners practice AI-assisted marketing content generation                        |
| **Customer Service & Engagement**         | 20 min   | Demo: ChatGPT chatbot, HubSpot AI                                                                   | Learners understand AI for automated responses, personalization, churn prediction |
| **Finance & Operations**                  | 30 min   | Hands-on: QuickBooks AI, Excel Copilot financial forecasts                                          | Learners practice automating reports, predicting cash flow, spotting anomalies    |
| **Product Development & Ideation**        | 20 min   | Activity: Figma AI mockup, ChatGPT for feature suggestions                                          | Learners explore AI for prototyping, user feedback analysis, scenario simulation  |
| **Time Management & Workflow Automation** | 15 min   | Demo: Notion AI Planner, Trello AI automation                                                       | Learners optimize schedules and track KPIs with AI                                |
| **Activity — Task Evaluation**            | 20 min   | Individual/group: List recurring tasks, apply AI suitability criteria                               | Learners identify high-value AI use cases for their business                      |
| **Case Studies Practice**                 | 45 min   | Breakout groups: Apply AI to real scenarios (Pitch prep, Marketing campaign, Financial forecasting) | Learners practice generating AI outputs for actionable business decisions         |
| **Wrap-up & Cheat Sheet**                 | 15 min   | Recap key points, distribute one-page cheat sheet                                                   | Learners leave with a concise AI reference for entrepreneurship                   |

---

## **Materials Needed**

* Laptops or tablets with internet access
* Accounts for AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Jasper AI, Figma AI, QuickBooks AI)
* Projector or screen for demos
* Handouts: AI for Entrepreneurs cheat sheet, task evaluation worksheet

---

## **Assessment / Evaluation**

* Observe group activity participation and AI tool outputs
* Review task evaluation tables for understanding of AI suitability
* Check case study solutions for practical application of AI

---

## **Homework / Follow-Up**

* Apply AI tools to one real business task (e.g., draft marketing emails, run competitor analysis, create a cash flow forecast)
* Document AI-assisted workflow and insights for reflection

---

## **Notes for Instructor**

* Emphasize that AI **augments** rather than replaces human judgment
* Encourage learners to balance automation with strategic thinking
* Highlight ethical, privacy, and compliance considerations in AI use
