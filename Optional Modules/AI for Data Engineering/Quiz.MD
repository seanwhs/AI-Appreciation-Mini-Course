## **Section 4 ‚Äî Application / Scenario (3 Questions)**

1. You notice AI flags anomalies in a batch data pipeline. Describe how a data engineer should respond.
   **Sample Answer:** Review AI alerts, investigate potential causes, validate against historical patterns, correct data or pipeline issues, and document resolution

2. A team wants to quickly onboard a new dataset from multiple sources. How can AI assist?
   **Sample Answer:** Detect source schemas, suggest field mappings and transformations, highlight inconsistencies, and generate metadata documentation

3. During an AI-guided exploration session, the engineer observes unusual distributions in a table. What steps should follow?
   **Sample Answer:** Investigate anomalies, confirm data integrity, adjust transformations if necessary, document findings, and inform stakeholders
