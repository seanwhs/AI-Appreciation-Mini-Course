## **Section 1: Multiple Choice**

**1. What is a primary benefit of using AI in marketing?**
a) AI can replace all human creativity
b) AI can automate repetitive tasks and provide data-driven insights
c) AI can guarantee viral campaigns
d) AI can create a brand identity from scratch

**Answer:** b) AI can automate repetitive tasks and provide data-driven insights

---

**2. Which task still requires human judgment when using AI in marketing?**
a) Scheduling social media posts
b) Analyzing historical engagement data
c) Ensuring content aligns with brand voice and ethics
d) Generating email subject lines

**Answer:** c) Ensuring content aligns with brand voice and ethics

---

**3. AI can help marketers with:**
a) Customer segmentation and targeting
b) Content generation for blogs and social media
c) Campaign performance tracking and optimization
d) All of the above

**Answer:** d) All of the above

---

**4. Which of the following AI tools is used primarily for audience segmentation?**
a) Jasper.ai
b) HubSpot
c) Canva AI
d) ChatGPT

**Answer:** b) HubSpot

---

**5. Predictive analytics in AI marketing is used to:**
a) Guarantee campaign success
b) Forecast potential outcomes and trends
c) Replace human decision-making
d) Automatically generate brand values

**Answer:** b) Forecast potential outcomes and trends
