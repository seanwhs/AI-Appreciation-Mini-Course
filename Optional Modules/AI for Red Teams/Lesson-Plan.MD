# **Lesson Plan: AI for Red Teams**

**Target Audience:** Authorized red-team operators, penetration testers (ethical), security engineers
**Duration:** 2–3 hours (adjustable)
**Delivery Mode:** Instructor-led, hybrid, or online

---

## **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

1. Describe how AI supports red-team workflows ethically and safely.
2. Identify AI tools and platforms that accelerate reconnaissance, modeling, and reporting.
3. Apply AI to scenario planning, adversary emulation, and engagement documentation.
4. Understand the limits of AI and the necessity of human oversight in red-team operations.
5. Evaluate which tasks are suitable for AI augmentation based on efficiency, repetitiveness, and data intensity.

## **Learning Activities**

1. **Interactive Discussion:** Identify repetitive, documentation-heavy, or planning-intensive red-team tasks suitable for AI support.
2. **Hands-On Demo:** AI-assisted reconnaissance summarization and attack path modeling (safe, in-scope examples).
3. **Group Exercise:** Scenario building with AI-generated adversary profiles.
4. **Workshop:** AI-assisted reporting and executive summary drafting.
5. **AI Suitability Assessment:** Evaluate 5–7 recurring red-team tasks using the AI potential table.

---

## **Assessment & Evaluation**

* **Formative:** Group discussions, scenario workshops, AI-assisted exercises.
* **Summative:** Multiple-choice or scenario-based quiz on AI application in red teaming.
* **Practical Evaluation:** Completion of AI opportunity table and suggested workflow improvements.

---

## **Materials & Resources**

* Slides / visual deck for AI in red teaming
* Access to AI-enabled simulation, planning, and reporting tools (Maltego AI, Caldera, PlexTrac AI, Notion AI, Cortex XSOAR)
* Collaboration tools (Miro, MURAL)
* Cheat sheet: “AI in Red Teaming” one-page reference
* Sample recon data and sanitized exercise telemetry for hands-on exercises

---

## **Follow-Up & Reinforcement**

* Encourage participants to integrate AI into scenario planning, modeling, and documentation in ongoing authorized engagements.
* Distribute one-page AI cheat sheet for reference.
* Schedule follow-up sessions to evaluate AI adoption and efficiency improvements in exercises.
