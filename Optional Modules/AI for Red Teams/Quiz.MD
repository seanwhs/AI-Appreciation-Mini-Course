## **Section 3 ‚Äî Short Answer (5 Questions)**

1. Name two AI tools used for red-team recon or scenario planning.
   **Sample Answer:** Recorded Future, MITRE Caldera AI

2. How can AI help in attack-path modeling?
   **Sample Answer:** Suggests likely kill chains, identifies choke points, maps TTPs to MITRE ATT&CK, and aids in scenario narrative generation

3. What are the benefits of AI-assisted reporting for red teams?
   **Sample Answer:** Reduces time spent drafting reports, maintains consistency, produces executive summaries, creates evidence tables, and maps findings to ATT&CK

4. How does AI support safe red-team infrastructure management?
   **Sample Answer:** Suggests safe deployment options, generates synthetic payloads, monitors exposure, and ensures operational security (OPSEC)

5. Why is human involvement essential in AI-driven red-team workflows?
   **Sample Answer:** Humans perform exploit execution, strategic decisions, ethical oversight, ensure compliance with ROE, and validate AI outputs

## **Section 4 ‚Äî Scenario / Application (3 Questions)**

1. AI highlights potential detection gaps during a red-team engagement. What steps should you take?
   **Sample Answer:** Review AI-identified gaps, validate with logs and telemetry, prioritize findings for reporting, communicate with blue team for feedback

2. AI generates draft reports from exercise logs. How do you ensure accuracy and relevance?
   **Sample Answer:** Verify AI-generated content against actual actions, check ATT&CK mappings, include human narrative and context, and review for executive readability

3. AI suggests improvements for purple-team collaboration. How should red-teamers act on this?
   **Sample Answer:** Integrate AI suggestions into next exercises, validate against ROE, adjust scenarios, and use as training material for junior staff
