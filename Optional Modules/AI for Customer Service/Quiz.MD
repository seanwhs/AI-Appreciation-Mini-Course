**2. AI can help customer service agents by:**
a) Replacing human judgment completely
b) Routing tickets to the correct department
c) Always resolving complex escalations

**Answer:** b) Routing tickets to the correct department

---

**3. Which of these is NOT a common AI application in customer service?**
a) Summarizing call transcripts
b) Detecting customer sentiment
c) Writing personal handwritten thank-you notes
d) Suggesting solutions from a knowledge base

**Answer:** c) Writing personal handwritten thank-you notes

---

**4. When should a human agent take over instead of AI?**
a) Responding to common shipping inquiries
b) Customer expresses anger or frustration
c) Checking order status

**Answer:** b) Customer expresses anger or frustration

---

**5. AI in customer service helps improve:**
a) Speed of responses

**Answer:** d) All of the above
