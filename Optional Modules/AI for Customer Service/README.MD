# **AI in Customer Service ‚Äî The Big Picture**

Customer service teams manage many responsibilities including answering inquiries, troubleshooting issues, processing requests, documenting cases, and communicating clearly. Much of this work involves:

* **Repetition** (common questions, password resets, tracking updates)
* **Information retrieval** (policies, product details, account data)
* **Time-sensitive tasks** (escalations, SLAs, complaint handling)
* **Emotionally sensitive interactions** (angry or confused customers)
* **Documentation and summarization** (case notes, ticket logs)

AI helps reduce response times, improve answer accuracy, and support agents while preserving human empathy where it matters most.

### **AI automates:**

‚úì Answering FAQs
‚úì Drafting responses
‚úì Routing and prioritizing tickets
‚úì Summarizing calls and chats
‚úì Detecting sentiment or urgency
‚úì Suggesting solutions from a knowledge base
‚úì Translating messages
‚úì Predicting customer needs

### **Humans handle:**

* Emotional intelligence: empathy, active listening
* Complex or sensitive problem-solving
* Judgment in ambiguous situations
* Relationship building and tone calibration
* Final decisions on escalations

AI **supports** the agent‚Äînot replaces them.

# **1. Customer Inquiry Handling & Ticketing**

AI streamlines how customer requests are received, sorted, and triaged.

* Prioritizing angry or urgent customers
* Suggesting quick replies to save agent time
* Drafting professional, accurate responses

### **Real Tools**

Zendesk AI, Freshdesk, Intercom Fin, Salesforce Einstein, Ada

### **Platform Examples**

* Zendesk AI ticket classification
* Intercom‚Äôs automated resolution engine

AI analyzes customer data to predict trends and personalize experiences.

# **Module Summary**

Learners will understand:

* AI boosts efficiency in ticketing, drafting messages, and analysis.
* AI is best for repetitive, predictable, or information-heavy tasks.
* Human agents remain essential for emotional communication, judgment, and complex problem-solving.
* Combining AI with skilled customer service agents leads to higher satisfaction, reduced workload, and more consistent support experiences.

# üßæ **One-Page Cheat Sheet ‚Äî ‚ÄúAI for Customer Service‚Äù**

### **AI Excels At:**

* Drafting emails, chats, and responses
* Handling FAQs and simple workflows
* Routing tickets and identifying urgency
* Summarizing calls and customer history
* Providing recommended solutions
* Gathering customer information
* Translating messages instantly

### **When NOT to Use AI:**

* Sensitive conversations (complaints, emotional distress)
* Situations requiring empathy
* High-stakes financial or legal issues
* Complex troubleshooting requiring expertise
* Any scenario where inaccurate answers risk customer trust

### **Bottom Line:**

AI enhances speed, accuracy, and consistency‚Äîallowing customer service professionals to focus on **human connection, empathy, and problem-solving**.
