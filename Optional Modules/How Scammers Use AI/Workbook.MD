### **Section 2: Spotting AI-Driven Scams**

**2.1 Red Flags of AI Scams**
Scammers use AI to make their scams look real. Here are some signs to watch out for:

* **Unusual Language**: AI-generated texts often have odd grammar or strange phrasing.
* **Unexpected Requests**: Scammers often ask for money, personal information, or urgent action.
* **Fake Urgency**: Scammers may try to create a sense of urgency to rush you into making decisions.

### **Section 3: How to Protect Yourself from AI Scams**

**3.1 Simple Safety Steps**
Here are a few practical steps to keep yourself safe from AI scams:

* **Verify Contacts**: Always double-check if someone contacts you unexpectedly, especially with urgent requests.
* **Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Adds an extra layer of protection to your online accounts.
* **Stay Informed**: Regularly update yourself on new scams and online security threats.
