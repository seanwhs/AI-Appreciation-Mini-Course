### **2. Where AI Shows Up in Project Management**

Here we will dive into each aspect of project management and see how AI can streamline your tasks.

---

#### **2.1 Task Planning & Scheduling**

**What AI Does:**

* Predicts timelines and suggests task dependencies.
* Detects bottlenecks and resource conflicts.
* Recommends optimized schedules.
* Adjusts timelines dynamically based on progress.

**Use Cases:**

* Automatically reschedules when tasks are delayed.
* Optimizes resource allocation across projects.
* Identifies overcommitted team members.

**Tools:**

* Microsoft Project AI
* Asana Work Graph AI
* Monday.com AI
* ClickUp AI

---

**Reflection:**

* How do you currently plan and schedule tasks for a project?

### **5. One-Page Cheat Sheet: AI in Project Management**

**AI Excels At:**

* Task scheduling & optimization
* Resource balancing & workload prediction
* Risk identification & mitigation
* Reporting, dashboards, and KPIs
* Meeting summarization & action item generation
* Budget forecasting & “what-if” scenario analysis
* Process improvement & post-project analytics

**When Not to Use AI:**

* Critical decision-making without human review
* Negotiation with stakeholders
* Creative problem-solving

**Outcome:**
Project managers can **spend less time on administration** and **more time on strategy, leadership, and team alignment**.
